About us

Experience and precision
in every project

We are a Structural Design Office specializing in the design of reinforced concrete structures for residential, commercial and industrial buildings. We specialize in precast elements.

Thanks to our extensive experience, we can offer not only the optimization of a given building, but also the conversion of its structure to precast construction.

In our daily work, we use BIM software, which allows us to collaborate seamlessly with other disciplines. Our 3D models contain all the information needed at each stage of the investment.

Precast Structures

Design of structural elements: columns, beams, foundation walls, stairs, walls and prestressed girders. Documentation of filigree, hollow-core and prestressed floor slabs.

Reinforced Concrete Structures

Technical and detailed designs for all types of reinforced concrete structures. Conversion from monolithic to precast construction while maintaining full efficiency.

Technological designs and prestressing programs for post-tensioned and pretensioned structures. Workshop designs for pretensioned elements.
